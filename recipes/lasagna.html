Lasagna

Description

A delicious, easy to make lasagna recipe.

Perfect for those days when you just want a delicious pasta dish

Ingredients

  • 15 oz. ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • Sauce
  • Lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Make sauce
  3. Layer sauce, pasta, cheese
