Macaroni and Cheese

Description

A cheesy baked mac and cheese recipe you will love.

Easy to make and makes for great leftovers.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Milk
  • Bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Boil Macaroni
  2. Make cheese sauce
  3. Mix sauce and macaroni
  4. Melt butter and stir in bread crumbs
  5. Top macaroni with brea crumb mixture and bake for 30 mins
